Alone in Berlin

A new zine1 about loneliness, depression, and Berlin.

In 2017 I made a big move but things didn't go so well. One positive I can take from this experience is the things I've learned about myself.

This publication explores themes of loneliness, depression, and the subsequent self-care that followed2.
